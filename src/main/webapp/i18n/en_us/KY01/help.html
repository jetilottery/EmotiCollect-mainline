EmotiCollect

Have a poppin’ good time as you collect emoticons to win big prizes! Spin the wheel and watch as matching emoticons pop and fill the emotion meter. Fill a meter to win! When the wheel lands on a Star symbol, pick a Star card for an instant win prize! Some emoticons may reveal extra spins for more chances to win!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Each game consists of 5 wheel spins. Press SPIN to spin the wheel to collect an Emoticon symbol or an Instant Win Star symbol.

Collecting an Emoticon symbol causes all matching emoticons on the grid to explode and fill a portion of the corresponding Emoticon meter. Fill a meter to win a prize.

As Emoticons explode from the grid, some reveal an Extra Spin symbol, which adds one additional spin to the Spin meter.

Collect an Instant Win Star symbol to choose an instant win card. Pick a card and win the associated prize.

The game ends when all spins have been completed. A message appears, showing the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Gameplay continues until all spins are completed.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
